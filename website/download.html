Getting started

Playing in four steps.

1 · Sign in

Launch Omega and sign in with the Microsoft account that owns Minecraft. It works out of the box — no setup, and tokens are encrypted on your machine.

2 · Create an instance

Point New Instance at your existing .minecraft folder — or pick an empty one and let Omega download Minecraft + Fabric/Forge fresh from Mojang.

3 · Import your mods

Select your mod .jars once. They show up as toggle switches with real names, versions, and auto-detected tags. Flip freely or use a preset.

4 · Press Play

Omega verifies the install, builds the launch, and streams the console live. In-game, press Right Shift for every built-in feature.
